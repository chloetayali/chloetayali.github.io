Decolonising the Archive

DTA have recently received control of the Olive Morris Archive and I'm helping them find ways to celebrate her legacy.

We have an ever-evolving online archive and we're working on a podcast looking at what today's activists can learn from Olive's tactics.

Our predecessors, the Remembering Olive Collective, established an award in Olive's name. We're celebrating this year's awardees with a free event on Friday the 25th at the Karibu Centre. There'll be poetry, film, hot food and Sistah Space's Ngozi Fulani as our keynote speaker.
